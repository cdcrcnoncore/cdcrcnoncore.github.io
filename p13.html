Can you give a general discription of the interview with some interview questions?

Round 1 : Was divided into 2 sections. The first section had calculation questions. They were about 30 questions in 5-8 minutes don't remember the time but it was very less. The second section had 45 Logical Reasoning questions in about 30 minutes.
Round 2 : It was a Skype interview. I was asked the following questions - 1. Reason for joining FF 2. What is Repo rate and reverse repo rate. 3. MCLR and SLR. 4. 2008 Economic crisis vs Great Depression 5. Differences between Central Bank and commercial bank. 6. My SWOT analysis. 7. My family background. 8. A question on long distance relationships vs normal ones. 9. Instances of handling pressure. 10. I work better in groups or individually.
Round 3: We were called to the Gurgaon office.The first round there was a rapid fir Logical reasoning questions round. We were taken into a room and we were asked about 20-25 questions of good logical reasoning skills. Only the first one to answer got the points either positive for correct answers or negative for wrong answers. No marks for others.
Round 4: Interview with the manager. Was asked basic HR questions. 1. Where do I see myself in 5 years. 2. Questions on handling stress. 3. Why FF? 4. Was told to describe VORP model of sports statistics as I had mentioned sports as my interest. 5. Questions on family. 6. Favorite market to trade in. 7. Past experience in trading.


How did you prepare for it?

The prep required you to have good and very fast calculation skills. Along with that you needed to be good in LR skills.
For calculation practice you can visit rankyourbrain.com and since I was preparing for CAT I didn't do much for LR.


Any tips for juniors?

1. Be calm and patient. 2. Don't fret too much over written tests. Sleep well before the test so that you are mentally at peak while attempting the paper. 3. Practice well for the written tests and try to increase your speed. 4 Interview might become grilling don't show them that you are scared even if you are wrong or they are grilling your.
